What it does

Three steps. Zero paperwork.

Built around the actual workflow of Philippine notaries — not generic e-signature tools.

PDF Intake

Drop a notarized PDF. We read the document type, principals, and contact emails automatically. SC-style filename auto-generated.

  • · OCR-extracted metadata
  • · Auto-rename per your convention
  • · Stored privately, scoped to your account

Notarial Register

Doc No., Page No., Book No., Series of — the 2004 Notarial Rules format, automated. Counters advance per entry. CSV export for archive.

  • · Auto-incrementing per lawyer
  • · Search, filter, monthly view
  • · Export-ready

OCS Dispatch

Auto-drafts emails to your client and the Office of the Clerk of Court. Review, edit, send — or batch them up for end-of-day.

  • · Pre-filled register details
  • · BCC firm archive
  • · Audit-logged

How it works

Notarize, log, dispatch — all in one flow.

1

Upload the notarized PDF

Drop the file in. We auto-detect document type, principals, and email contacts.

2

Verify the extracted data

Edit any field if needed. Date, fee, principals — all in one screen.

3

Log to your register

Doc No., Page, Book, Series — all auto-incremented. SC-style filename generated.

4

Dispatch to client + OCS

Pre-drafted emails, attached PDF. Send now or queue for batch dispatch.
